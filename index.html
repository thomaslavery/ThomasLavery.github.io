kLOC Club

What is kLOC Club?

The kLOC Club is an initiative to create a community to share code. The goal is to publicly share and review the last thousand lines (or however many since the last review) of code that you wrote. This review should include what you did, why you did it, and what you learned. Other people in the community are encouraged to review the other peoples code and provide constructive feedback.

Purpose

The primary purpose of the kLOC Club is to practice reviewing your's and other's code while learning new techniques and practices. The secondary purpose is to further contribute to the open source community. Finally, I believe that cognitive load is a key factor in knowledge retention. With the contemporary solution space being largely dominated by AI. kLOC Club serves as a way to constructively increase cognitive load through spaced repetition.
